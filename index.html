DeepChIA-PET

DeepChIA-PET is a supervised deep learning method for prediciting ChIA-PET from Hi-C and ChIP-seq. It used a deep dilated residual network to learn the mapping relationships between ChIA-PET and two widely-used methods (Hi-C and ChIP-seq).
